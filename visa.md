## Visa Requirements for Visiting {{ site.country }}

---

All participants should verify their visa requirements prior to attending the conference. Requirements vary by citizenship and passport type.

For official information, please consult the [Hellenic Republic Ministry of Foreign Affairs](https://www.mfa.gr/en/services/visas-for-foreigners-traveling-to-greece/countries-requiring-or-not-requiring-a-visa/) website to check if your country requires a visa. Please note that you must complete registration to be able to obtain a visa support letter.
