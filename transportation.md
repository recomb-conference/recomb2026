### Metro
{: style="text-align: center;"}

The metro line connects key areas including the city center, the railway station, and major landmarks.

| Ticket Type | Full Price | Reduced Price |
|-------------|------------|---------------|
| Basic Ticket (one journey) | €0.60 | €0.30 |
| 10+1 Basic Tickets | €5.80 | €2.90 |
| Daily Ticket (24 hours, unlimited rides) | €2.50 | – |

**Children under 6** travel free when accompanied by an adult with a valid ticket.

**How to buy tickets:**
- Purchase tickets at **automated vending machines** available at all metro stations
- All tickets and personalized cards can be reloaded at the vending machines
- Keep your receipt as proof of purchase

**Validation:**
- Validate your ticket **twice**: once when entering and once when exiting through the gates
- Place your ticket/card on the designated reader at the validation machines

**More info:** [Thessaloniki Metro Official Website](https://www.thessmetro.gr/en/), [Tickets](https://www.thessmetro.gr/en/tickets/)

### Public Bus (OASTH)
{: style="text-align: center;"}

Thessaloniki's public transportation is operated by **OASTH** (Thessaloniki Urban Transport Organization). Buses cover the entire city and surrounding areas.

| Ticket Type | Price | Reduced Price |
|-------------|-------|---------------|
| Urban Zone Ticket (70 mins) | €0.60 | €0.30 |
| Peri-Urban Zone Ticket (70 mins) | €0.80 | €0.40 |
| Package of 10+1 Urban Zone Tickets (70 mins) | €5.80 | €2.90 |
| Package of 10+1 Peri-Urban Zone Tickets (70 mins) | €7.80 | €3.90 |
| Urban and Peri-Urban Zone Day Ticket | €2.50 | - |
| Special Bus Routes Ticket (airport bus services and Cultural Line, 70 mins) | €2.00 | €1.00 |

**How to pay:**
- Purchase a **ThessTicket** rechargeable card from kiosks or the OASTH office
- Tickets can also be purchased on board
- Validate your ticket when boarding by tapping on the validator

**More info:** [OASTH Official Website](https://oasth.gr/), [Bus Tickets & Fares](https://oseth.com.gr/en/bus-fare-products)
